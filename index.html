CROstmas Cards is a collection on Cronos network. Collection size: 10 000, price: 10 CRO  To get your CROstmas Card connect with your Metamask wallet (must be on Cronos network). Enter amount you want to mint (max 50 per 1 transaction) and click mint.  You can also attach any amount of CRO to your CROstmas card and send it to your friends, which they will be able to "Unwrap" and get CRO to their wallet.

1 CROstmasCard costs 5 CRO
