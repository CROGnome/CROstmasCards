How do I mint CROstmas Cards?



To mint CROstmas Cards first you need metamask chrome extension. Then you need to connect to Cronos network (link how to do it bellow). Afterwards fill your wallet with CRO, head to main page. Enter amount of CROstmas Cards you want to mint and click mint. They should be visible shortly afterwards.

Adding Cronos to your metamask.

How do I gift CRO with my CROstmas Card?



On main page click "Show My Cards". Bellow each card you will find input field and a button. Enter amount of CRO you want to gift (must be integer) and click wrap.

After succesfully sending wrapping transaction, you can now gift CROstmas card with associated CRO. To do so enter wallet address of person to whom you want to gift it and card number in transfer section. Card number can be found just above your NFT.

When person recieves CROstmas card he can Unwrap it by clicking Unwrap button just below NFT.

How does wrapping CRO with CROstmas cards work?



When you Wrap CRO, amount of CRO along with token id of a card is sent to Wrap Card contract.

Each CROstmas Card id with mapped value of CRO wrapped is stored in contract where it can only be unwrapped by owner of that specific CROstmas Card.

So when you click Unwrap CRO that card no longer has any CRO assosiated with it and all previously assosiated CRO is sent to the owner of that CROstmas Card.
